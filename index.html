ABOUT ME

Stephanie is an aspiring UX/UI designer and developer currently a student at AkiraChix pursuing a Diploma in Information Technology. Her studies in this duration have built her foundation in UX/UI design and development enabling her to apply the skills in sketching wire frames using Adobe Illustrator, InDesign and Photoshop. She is also able to design and develop both static and responsive landing pages using HTML/CSS and JavaScript. Her ability to translate ideas into creative visuals through wireframes and mockups play a major role in the development of easy-to-use solutions. She continues to explore the design industry and update her projects to stay updated with current trends. She has always been fascinated by the visuals on cartoons and story books she read in her childhood. This has always seem to be her source of inspiration and excitement and it is the same reason why she would love to pursue a complementing career. When not a techie she enjoys painting, hiking, traveling around the world and doing DIYs. She recently developed an interest in reading novels after being recommended to read the novel Born a Crime by Trevor Noah. She continues to explore the creative world for source of extacy.
